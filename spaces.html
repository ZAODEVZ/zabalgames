What spaces are for

Each phase of ZABAL Gamez has live Farcaster spaces:

  • Launch spaces - the public announcement weekend (~May 24). Zaal walks through the format, DJs old ZAO music for lore, takes questions.
  • Workshop spaces - each June workshop is livestreamed first, then clipped and added to the Magnetiq library. Drop in live or watch on demand.
  • Builder spaces - during July's open build-a-thon, finalists share progress, work-in-public on stream. Casual, low-pressure.
  • Finals reveal stream - at T+72h on August Finals week, the WaveWarZ-Base settlement transaction fires and the placements are read out live.

How spaces happen on Farcaster

Farcaster spaces are live audio (or audio + video) sessions native to the protocol. Anyone with a Farcaster account can join - tap the live indicator on the /zabal channel, or watch the cast where the space was announced.

For workshop leads: when you record your June session, doing it as a Farcaster space first means it streams natively to /zabal, gets archived for replay, and the clip can be cross-posted by ZAO accounts immediately. Tools like Songjam can also transcribe.

For audience: follow /zabal to get notifications when a space goes live. You'll also see the announcement cast appear in the channel feed.
