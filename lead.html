Workshop Roster - Open

Lead a Workshop.

ZABAL Gamez Season 1 needs workshop leads. The workshops are a library of recorded sessions that get a whole cohort of Farcaster vibe-coders ready to ship. If you have built something worth teaching, you have a session to give.

The ask, in one paragraph

Record one ~30-minute session on your specialty. Livestream it on Farcaster spaces or screen-record it - either works. The clip lands in the workshop library on Magnetiq and stays available the whole cohort. That is the whole ask.

Not you, but you know someone?

There is a community wishlist of people we would love to see teach. Nominate someone by their GitHub, Farcaster, or X, upvote the ones you want, and tag them on Farcaster to invite them in.

See the Dream Leads board

What you teach

Topics are wide open. The cohort is Farcaster-native builders using AI tools. Pick what you actually know:

Empire Builder Clanker Farcaster mini apps Snaps Claude Code Cursor MCP agent harnesses tokens governance Respect + fractals music infra POIDH zlank SongJam festival ops your thing

What you get

Cast credit and a real hand in what The ZAO ships next

Public cast credit on launch and reveal, a first look at the strongest builds in the cohort, and a real hand in what The ZAO ships next.

Builder pipeline access

The cohort that watches your session is the same cohort that ships in July. If you are building something, this is direct top-of-funnel for the people most likely to plug into it.

Content asset

You walk away with one polished recording + a transcript + auto-generated clips for your own channels. ZAO accounts cross-amplify on Farcaster, X, Telegram, and the ZAO newsletter.
