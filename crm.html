The people

Everyone in the ZABAL Gamez orbit.

Two angles on the season's people. The Roster is everyone who led a workshop, presented, built, mentored, or was nominated, and what they were in for. The Players board is live: everyone earning points by joining, sharing, and showing up. Search, filter, and find their profiles and handles.

What do the levels mean?

Every card carries a Player Level - the headline of what someone did this season - plus a LVL number that blends that with live activity points.

  • Headliner - led a workshop or organized.
  • Builder - shipped a project.
  • Mentor - backing builders for the Finals.
  • Challenger - nominated to teach, not yet on stage.

The second line on each card is breadth: Triple threat, Double duty, or In the mix - how many ways someone showed up.
