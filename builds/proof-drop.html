A build-receipt kit for ZABAL Gamez. Make a portable receipt for shipped work, hash a proof photo locally so nothing private leaves your device, and export ready-to-post verification for the /zabal channel and the prize pot. It is a small tool that helps other builders prove what they shipped - which is exactly what July asks for.

What it does
  • Creates a portable build receipt for a live app, repo, demo, or mint page.
  • Hashes your proof photo locally with SHA-256 - the file never gets uploaded.
  • Generates copy-ready posts for the /zabal channel and X.
  • Links straight to the prize-pot claim so your entry is one flow.
