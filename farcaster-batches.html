Farcaster Batches - Day 1

The builders.

Farcaster Batches is a week-long builder showcase across the Farcaster ecosystem, organized by JubJub and hosted by the GM Farcaster team (Adriene and Nounish Prof), with Adrian (diviflyy) of Empire Builder co-hosting. Day 1 brought five builders on stage to break down what they are building and how it connects. The full session is captured as a ZABAL data stream. Here is each project, on one page.

Vinny App POIDH Juke Founder Check Fotocaster email remittance pro Crafters Ghostwriter

What Farcaster Batches is

A week-long focus on the builders around the Farcaster ecosystem - builders present live, with two workshops landing later in the week (a Founder Check workshop in the GM Farcaster studio, and Cashless Man and Royal Aid on AI automation and self-hosted infrastructure). The throughline every speaker and Adrian keep returning to: build the thing you want to see in the world.

Day 1 aired Jun 2, 2026. Read the full cleaned transcript or see it in the Zabal Data Streams feed.

The projects

project

Vinny App

Chris Dolinsky (with Nick)

A natural-language app builder on a Web3 stack: prompt a fully functional application into existence in minutes, in any language, no coding required. It sits on top of harnesses like Claude Code and Codex and layers on the connections, wallets, and primitives you would otherwise provision yourself. Every app gets its own EVM wallet that pays for its AI build, storage, and image generation.

  • Vinny-a-thon: $1,000 USDC prize pool (extra $250 from Justin / AHN.eth). Build Jun 1-5, judging Jun 5.
  • Optional Clanker token per app; 10% of token trading fees roll up into the umbrella Vinny token.
  • Composes with Empire Builder (yerbearserker / Diviflyy). Full leaderboard; tracks unique users per app.
  • Distribution: World, Celo MiniPay, Telegram (coming), and the open web.
project

POIDH

Kenny

A fully on-chain social bounty platform: crowdfund any outcome, verify it happened, all trustlessly. Each bounty is an "ephemeral DAO" with a small attack surface - one specific yes/no outcome that the people who funded it vote on. Three years building in public on Farcaster.

  • Now live on Ethereum mainnet (contracts tested).
  • Batches promo: the first 20 Farcaster-tied bounties get a $25 boost plus promotion. Minimum bounty 0.001 ETH.
  • Signature win: a $30,000 Farcaster-crowdfunded kickflip world record (Dave Bachinsky). See Kenny's article "The Ephemeral DAO Machine."
poidh.xyz
project

Juke

Nikki Sapp

An open-source, mobile-native, audio-first Farcaster client. Built in about a week to push for native audio Spaces - which Farcaster (Rish) later shipped. Juke keeps going as the open-source baseline client (point an LLM at the repo and build your own) plus features Farcaster lacks: voice notes posted directly to the feed, Space recording, and a new audio SDK that Zaal helped test end to end.

  • Audio SDK: integrate native Farcaster audio Spaces directly into your own site or app.
  • Deliberately no token and no wallet to launch one - a passion project, open to contributors.
  • Supports mini apps, snaps, channels, and a GIF keyboard.
project

Founder Check

Jonathan Colton

An agent-driven go-to-market validation mini app, grown out of the book "Distribution Is Hard, Don't F It Up." It runs GTM pain research with agents and scores how clearly a founder can communicate an idea - "Duolingo for builders," where you use it, improve your score, and get better at being understood and replicated.

  • Built with the Farcaster building agent (built-in wallet, social graph attached).
  • A live session with Zaal jumped his score 3.3 points out of 10 - one of the top jumps measured.
  • Live-workshop format: run one idea through it on stream and watch the score reveal.
project

Fotocaster

Jonathan Colton and Erik

A photo mint built for maximum artist sovereignty. Born from frustration with Rodeo's 50% take and lack of control over editions, Fotocaster lets artists keep nearly all of their proceeds while the platform takes about a penny per sale. Mostly photography, inexpensive, a joyful way to collect art on the timeline.

  • Over 6,000 collects in the first 30 days.
  • Built with the Farcaster building agent; Erik was the architect.
project

email remittance pro

Dr. Deeks

Send crypto to anyone by email - the email is the identity layer, no required identity verification. The sender funds an escrow wallet; the recipient clicks a claim link and chooses how to receive it: the token sent, a different token across Monad, Base, or Celo, or a gift card, with any swap or bridging handled on the back end. Built for the Synthesis hackathon, fully autonomous-agent-created.

  • Two modes - business and personal. 1.5% flat fee on funds sent in; everything else to the user.
  • Proven on chain (transaction proofs + video). Redeploying on Render and Cloudflare.
project

Crafters

Dr. Deeks

An AI word-combination game. Start with a base set - water, earth, air, fire - and drag two words together; AI combines them into a new word recorded in the database. The first person ever to create a given word can mint a Megamind NFT marking them as its creator.

  • Beta live as a mini app / web app.
  • Agent-driven features in progress: Megamind heists, rival coaching, rentable agents that keep crafting on your behalf, daily challenges, on-chain check-ins, leaderboards, and a dynamic celestial weather system.
project

Ghostwriter

Dr. Deeks

A community-driven, Madlib-style storytelling game. Contribute a word into an active story to earn a creator credit, which lets you start your own story from a prompt and category. Each submitted word mints an NFT with hidden metadata; when the story completes, it reveals automatically and everyone sees what they built together.

  • One of several Dr. Deeks projects (alongside Journey Through Time).
