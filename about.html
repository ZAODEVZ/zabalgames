Who is Behind ZABAL Gamez

Built by the ZAO.

ZABAL Gamez Season 1 is hosted by Zaal under the BetterCallZaal umbrella, run by a small core of ZAO ecosystem builders, and powered by partner platforms across Farcaster, Base, and Solana. Below is the cast list.

Hosts

ORGANIZER

Zaal - @bettercallzaal, FID 19640

The organizer. Runs ZABAL Gamez, BetterCallZaal, the /zabal channel. Builder, music infra, ZAO operations. Site: bettercallzaal.com.

COMMUNITY

The ZAO

100+ member gated Farcaster music community. Governance by Respect (soulbound, peer-validated, earned through weekly fractal sessions). The constituency the Games serves and the voter set for the Finals.

Core Builders

WAVEWARZ FOUNDER

Hurric4n3Ike - @hurric4n3ike on X

Built WaveWarZ on Solana - the artist prediction-market platform. Growing battle history + cumulative SOL volume (live numbers at wavewarz-intelligence.vercel.app). WaveWarZ is the underlying tech the August Finals will run on (per Doc 720).

WAVEWARZ-BASE

Sam - WaveWarZ-Base co-builder

Shipping the Base agentic version of WaveWarZ alongside Hurric4n3Ike. Two contracts on testnet, mainnet-bound for July. Full handle + surname pending confirmation.

ZAO MUSIC + DEVOPS

Iman

ZAO Music team member, working with Zaal on the cowork action-item tracker, ZOE skills coordination, ORDAO governance integration for the Respect-holder snapshot mechanism.
