The recordings

Every session, recorded and kept.

One front door to everything we capture - the ZABAL Gamez workshops, the Farcaster Batches showcases, the pre-FarHack agentic bootcamp the rails trace back to, and the long-form interviews. Recorded, transcribed, and kept, so missing it live never means missing it. It all lands in Bonfire, the ZAO memory layer, so any builder or agent can query the whole record.

Farcaster Batches

GM Farcaster's week-long builder showcases, organized by JubJub, hosted by GM Farcaster (Adriene and Nounish Prof).

Builders across the Farcaster ecosystem break down what they are building. Day 1 brought Vini App, POIDH, Juke, Founder Check, Fotocaster and Dr. Deeks; Day 3 brought ZABAL Gamez, Empire Builder, Defense of the Agents, Booster and Celebration Hub. GM Farcaster ran the week and hosts every episode publicly - on top of their videos we add a summary and full transcript for each day.

origin

Farcaster Agentic Bootcamp (pre-FarHack)

Two-week program co-hosted by Builders Garden and Dev3Pack - zero to autonomous agents and mini apps.

The origin thread. The bootcamp fed FarHack Online, where Zaal and Zlank won the Snaps category - and that prize seeded the ZABAL Gamez pool. The agent and mini-app patterns taught here are the same rails ZABAL Gamez runs on.
