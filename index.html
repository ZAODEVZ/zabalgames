Questions, answered

The honest answers to what people actually ask before joining.

Do I need to be a developer?

No. Any harness counts - vibe-coding, no-code, design, writing, community work. The June workshops bring you up to speed on the tools and the ecosystem before July starts.

How much time does this take?

As little or as much as you want. The July build can be a single weekend. Workshops are recorded, so you watch live or anytime. Pick one door - you don't have to do all three.

What if I don't finish?

You keep everything you built and learned. Finishing unlocks the pay and a commemorative collectible, but there's no penalty for starting and no cost to trying.

Is it really free?

Yes. No entry fee, no token you have to buy, no catch. Free to join and free workshops, open to anyone.

How is this different from a weekend hackathon?

You build for a real 100+ member community that needs the tools, on live rails, with a ZAO mentor as your teammate - and you keep the repo plus the trade cut after the Finals. It doesn't disappear when the weekend ends.

Who is behind ZABAL Gamez?

The ZAO - a Farcaster-native community that has run weekly Respect sessions for 90+ weeks straight since August 2024. Builders from the ecosystem won the Snap category at FarHack 2026.
