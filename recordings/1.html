Don't launch a token yet - from dream to empire.

yerbearserker · @yerbearserker · Empire Builder · Day 1, builder track - live workshop, recorded

Watch Part 2 Read the transcript The ZABAL Gamez Empire

The first official ZABAL Gamez workshop. BetterCallZaal sits down with yerbearserker (Jordan Oram), co-founder and chief ecosystem architect of Empire Builder, on why most projects launch token-first, spike, get extracted, and collapse - and what to do instead: build a real project and community first, then let the token amplify it. He walks through the Empire V3 no-code tool stack and the Triple A path, and stands up a tokenless ZABAL Gamez empire live. This is Part 1, the framework. The live demo is Part 2.

Chapters
Topics
Empire Builder tokenless Empire Triple A framework leaderboards boosters staking tactical generosity distribution
Key takeaways
  • Do not launch a token until you are ready - build the foundation first.
  • The token is the amplifier, not the foundation. Sequence matters.
  • The Triple A framework: Assemble (build, no token), Affirm (reward your active people), Ascend (launch the token once the ecosystem can carry it).
  • Leaderboards, boosters, staking, and tactical generosity power a tokenless empire.
  • yerbearserker stood up a tokenless ZABAL Gamez empire live during the session.
