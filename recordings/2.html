Part 2 of the first ZABAL Gamez workshop - the build that follows the thesis. After the Part 1 framework talk (don't launch a token yet), yerbearserker (Jordan Oram) of Empire Builder gives a hands-on tour of the Empire tool stack from inside Farcaster, walks the top empires and how leaderboards score holders times boosters, runs a live Warplet raffle airdrop to DotA NFT holders, adds a $Zabal token booster, and stands up a fresh tokenless ZABAL Gamez empire on the spot - then wires two leaderboards: /zabal channel activity and cast reactions.

Key takeaways
  • See a tokenless empire stood up live, start to finish.
  • Leaderboards score a base component (NFTs or tokens held) multiplied by boosters - NFT holdings, token thresholds, or staking.
  • Distribution in practice: a live Warplet raffle airdrop to the most active DotA NFT holders.
  • A $Zabal token booster added live (10M minimum, 3x), funded from the treasury.
  • Your main empire is the tree, sub-empires are the branches - a generative, connected ecosystem.
