Zaal's weekly introduction to the ZAO Fractal, framed as the first ZAO project you can build on. He covers what the ZAO is - a decentralized impact network for the creator economy, the Z Talent Artist Organization - and how it runs on soulbound, illiquid ZAO Respect earned weekly through peer consensus. He walks the four objectives (empowerment, transparency, sustainability, equitable distribution) and the Respect game itself: breakout groups of up to six, four-minute updates, then on-chain consensus. The ZAO Fractal meets weekly at 6pm Eastern at discord.theZAO.com.

Key takeaways
  • The ZAO is a decentralized impact network for the creator economy, governed by soulbound, illiquid ZAO Respect earned weekly through peer consensus - not bought.
