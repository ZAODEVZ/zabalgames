ZABAL GAMEZ Workshop w/diviflyy from Empire Builder

Adrian (diviflyy) · @diviflyy · Empire Builder · Builder track - Conversation + live demo, recorded

Watch on YouTube Read the transcript Empire Builder

Adrian (diviflyy), co-founder and lead engineer of Empire Builder, goes under the hood of Empire Builder V3 - the docs at empirebuilder.world, the public free-to-use endpoints, and how to pull leaderboards, rankings, boosters, and reward data into your own apps. Empire Builder is the community-layer framework for any project, token-based or tokenless. Live, Adrian builds a Zabal Empire mini app in about an hour using the Empire Builder skill plus a free Farcaster data source - surfacing rewards, rankings, and boosters under your own brand. He covers getting your empire ID, the endpoint set, and where it is heading: multiple treasuries, more chains, and agent bots that run an empire. Plus build ideas for the ZABAL Gamez hackathon.

Key takeaways
  • Empire Builder is a community-layer framework for any project, token-based or tokenless.
  • The public, free-to-use endpoints surface top empires, boosters, distributions, and leaderboards - no key needed.
  • Your empire ID is your token address, or an address-based identifier for tokenless empires.
  • You can build a branded rewards mini app in about an hour with the Empire Builder skill and an AI assistant.
  • Big build ideas: a tag-to-query empire bot, an Empire Draft game, and one-shot tokenless empire deploys.
