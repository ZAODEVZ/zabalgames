A ZABAL Gamez conversation with Adam "The Thriller" Miller, founder and CEO of MIDAO. Two threads: MIDAO, the Marshall Islands legal framework that lets a DAO - or now a decentralized AI agent - become a real legal entity (a DAO LLC), so the people governing it are not personally liable for what it does; and Beta Briefing, his agentic daily-news tool that runs a multi-stage AI pipeline over your interests and delivers an editorialized briefing in text and audio. Plus a practical agentic-engineering segment: directing Claude Code with Codex as a second opinion and a dual-review sub-agent command.

Topics
MIDAO DAO LLC AI agent legal entity Marshall Islands Beta Briefing agentic engineering Claude Code
Key takeaways
  • A DAO LLC (Marshall Islands) gives a DAO legal personhood and limited liability - the people governing it are not personally on the hook for the entity's actions.
  • The same structure now works for a decentralized AI agent: governed by people, but the agent is the manager - it can sign contracts and open bank accounts, and can be elected in or out.
  • If you do not elect a legal structure, the government applies a default one, usually not the one you want. Tokens and DAOs need documents.
  • Beta Briefing runs an 11-plus stage agentic pipeline over your profile to deliver a daily, editorialized news briefing in text and audio (RSS too).
  • Agentic-engineering tip: have Claude Code call Codex (and Google's CLI) for second opinions, build a dual-review sub-agent command, and keep upgrading your Claude.md and skills so it runs longer autonomously.
