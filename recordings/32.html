ZABAL GAMEZ Workshop w/Ceci from Unlock Protocol

Ceci Sakura · Unlock Protocol · Builder track - Live talk plus Q and A, recorded

Technical4/10Some technical depth
Watch on YouTube Read the transcript

Ceci Sakura, marketing and communications steward at Unlock Protocol, walks through what Unlock is and demos it live. Unlock is an open-source protocol that lets creators and communities issue their own memberships, subscriptions, event tickets, and certifications as keys their audience actually owns, with no central platform in the middle. In the demo she deploys a certification on Base in real time, makes it non-transferable so it works as a soulbound proof, and airdrops it both to a wallet and to a plain email address, then shows the bulk CSV flow for a whole event. She and Zaal talk through using a certification as proof of a ZABAL Gamez submission, and she closes on how Unlock is run as a DAO and the open weekly calls anyone can join.

Key takeaways
  • Unlock lets you issue memberships, subscriptions, tickets, and certifications as keys your audience owns, with no central platform in the middle.
  • A certification lock can be made non-transferable, turning it into a soulbound proof someone completed or submitted something.
  • You can airdrop keys to a wallet or straight to an email address, so people new to this can still receive one.
  • Bulk-distribute with a CSV once you are an Unlock Prime member.
  • Unlock is run as a DAO with open weekly calls; contributing can start with content, bounties, or an idea.
