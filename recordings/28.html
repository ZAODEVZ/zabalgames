ZABAL GAMEZ Workshop w/Matt Lee from Tortoise

Matt Lee · @mattlee · Tortoise · Builder track - Live conversation plus Q and A, recorded

Technical5/10Some technical depth
Watch on YouTube Read the transcript

Matt Lee on Tortoise, and how musicians and creators can best use Farcaster. A musician and software engineer, he came up through the music industry and live sound before teaching himself to build, and got into crypto chasing one question - how to make a music industry where artists own and earn from their work. A hands-on look at building on Farcaster, finding your ideal user, and his snap bot.

Topics
Tortoise Music on Farcaster Building on Farcaster Creator economy Farcaster
Key takeaways
  • Owning digital media as the key to a healthier music industry - where that idea came from and why it matters.
  • Tortoise: giving musicians a place to build and grow on Farcaster.
  • Building on Farcaster - who your ideal user is, and what carries over for anyone building next.
  • A look at his snap bot.
