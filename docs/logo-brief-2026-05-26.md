# ZABAL Gamez Logo Brief - 10 Directions

> Brief for the designer. Pick any combo. Mix two. We want options.
> Drafted 2026-05-26.

## Context (give this to the designer)

- **What it is:** ZABAL Gamez Season 1 - a 3-month Farcaster vibe-coding challenge for the ZAO ecosystem. Streamer games crossed with a hackathon. June workshops + July build-a-thon + August Finals.
- **Parent brand:** The ZAO (music + web3 community, 100+ members, dark navy + gold/cyan accents).
- **Sibling brands:** WaveWarZ (music battles), COC Concertz (virtual concerts), ZAOstock (festival), BetterCallZaal (Zaal's personal).
- **Recurring format:** This is Season 1. Logo needs to work for Season 2, 3, 4 - season numeral might be a swappable element.
- **Audience:** Farcaster-native builders + ZAO music community + crypto-curious devs.
- **Personality:** Less "console gamer," more "builder showing up to play." Vibe-code is the medium - laptops + agents + Snap-builders, not Xbox controllers.

## The 10 directions

### 1. Arcade Cabinet Wordmark
- **Vibe:** 1982 arcade, Atari/Sega nostalgia
- **Visual:** Chunky beveled letters (ZABAL GAMEZ), drop shadow, optional CRT scanlines overlay
- **Reference:** Stranger Things arcade scenes, Reigns, The Last Starfighter
- **Why it fits:** "Streamer games" half of the pitch. Plays to nostalgia. Square format friendly.
- **Risk:** Too literal-game = might mislead vibe-coding builders into thinking it's a Twitch-only thing

### 2. Joystick Monogram (refined version of current submission)
- **Vibe:** Modern wordmark with one icon-substitution letter
- **Visual:** "Z[joystick]BAL GAMES" - the A is a stylized joystick. Cleaner geometry than current submission. Less red, more on-brand colors. Clearer that the joystick = A (not I).
- **Reference:** Twitch wordmark, Mixer, OBS letter substitution patterns
- **Why it fits:** Direct visual pun on Games. Recognizable. Easy avatar crop.
- **Risk:** A vs I ambiguity (your current logo has this issue). Designer must solve.

### 3. Pixel-Art Z (8-bit hero)
- **Vibe:** NES/MSX era pixel sprite
- **Visual:** Block-pixel "Z" mark in ZABAL signature color, with "GAMES" in clean modern sans below
- **Reference:** itch.io, Mario sprite typography, Pix Brix, Squarespace's Pixel templates
- **Why it fits:** Acknowledges gaming heritage without being literally a controller. Builder/maker feel.
- **Risk:** Pixel art is a crowded aesthetic in 2026; might feel generic without a twist

### 4. Builder/Terminal Mark
- **Vibe:** Vibe-coding native - half code editor, half game
- **Visual:** Wordmark in monospace (JetBrains Mono / IBM Plex Mono) with a blinking cursor `_` replacing one character, OR a `>` prompt prefix: `> ZABAL GAMEZ_`
- **Reference:** Vercel wordmark + cursor, Replit logo, Linear's monospace pull, Repl.it
- **Why it fits:** Vibe-coding is the actual medium. Builder DNA explicit. Reads as a CLI command which is the audience.
- **Risk:** Might feel too "dev tool" and miss the play/fun energy

### 5. Lightning-Bolt Z (energy + speed)
- **Vibe:** Speed, energy, season-of-shipping
- **Visual:** Stylized Z that doubles as a lightning bolt. Single bold mark. Works alone as icon.
- **Reference:** ZARA wordmark Z, Tesla T, Optimism Op-stack mark
- **Why it fits:** Z is the brand-shape across The ZAO ecosystem. Lightning = energy + the speed of vibe-coding + the streamer games element. Avatar-friendly.
- **Risk:** Generic-Z risk. Needs to feel distinctively ZABAL not generic web3.

### 6. Synthwave / Vaporwave Glow
- **Vibe:** Late-80s neon, Drive soundtrack, Stranger Things season 4
- **Visual:** Pink/cyan/purple gradient sunset-grid background, ZABAL GAMEZ in clean retro-futurist type centered, soft glow
- **Reference:** Stranger Things title cards, FM-84, The Midnight album art, ROY G BIV
- **Why it fits:** Music + games + community vibe. Photogenic on cast unfurls. Distinctive.
- **Risk:** Synthwave is overused 2020-2026. Need a twist to not feel dated.

### 7. Esports Badge / Crest
- **Vibe:** League shield, esports team identity
- **Visual:** Heraldic shield with ZABAL inside, GAMES as banner below, season ribbon at top, controllers/joysticks as supporters
- **Reference:** Cloud9, FaZe Clan, NRG Esports, Sentinels
- **Why it fits:** "Season 1" framing matches league/esports treatment. Permanent identity that scales across Seasons.
- **Risk:** Says "competitive esports team" not "build-in-public hackathon." Might mislead.

### 8. Minimalist Geometric Mark
- **Vibe:** Modern crypto/tech (think Coinbase, Base, Optimism)
- **Visual:** Single geometric mark - a rotated Z formed from triangles, or a hexagonal Z, or two interlocked Zs forming an infinity loop. Plus wordmark in clean grotesk.
- **Reference:** Optimism mark, Base mark, Linear, Vercel, Stripe
- **Why it fits:** Plays in the same visual league as Empire Builder, Clanker, Zora. Lives well in web3 wallets and Mini App tiles.
- **Risk:** Could feel cold or generic-crypto. Loses the "Games" energy.

### 9. Hand-Drawn / Sketch Energy
- **Vibe:** Indie game jam, itch.io, Are.na, post-Figma rebellion
- **Visual:** Hand-drawn-feel ZABAL GAMEZ wordmark with imperfect lines, ink-bleed details, sketch arrows pointing at things
- **Reference:** Are.na typography, Hundred Rabbits, Itch.io game jam pages, Notion's hand-drawn icons
- **Why it fits:** Counter-positions against polished VC-web3 brands. Says "small team, real humans, building in public." Matches Zaal's voice (caveman, terse, no fluff).
- **Risk:** Looks unprofessional in formal contexts (sponsor decks). Might not scale to tiny sizes.

### 10. Type-Only Wordmark (custom letterforms)
- **Vibe:** Wordmark-as-logo. No icon. Strong type does the work.
- **Visual:** Custom-cut ZABAL letterforms - maybe with a single chunky stroke variation that makes them feel coded/owned. GAMES as a smaller subtitle in a contrasting weight.
- **Reference:** Klim Type Foundry brand work, Helvetica Now applied logos, Dia Studio, Pentagram wordmarks
- **Why it fits:** Confident, brand-mature, doesn't compete with the joystick/controller iconography of literal gaming. Works at every size including 16x16 if the letterforms are clear enough.
- **Risk:** Doesn't say "Games" visually. Relies on the word doing the work.

## Recommended pairings (designer picks 2-3 to mock up)

If the designer can only do 2-3 mockups, suggest:

- **#2 (refined joystick) + #4 (builder/terminal)** - covers the gaming-fun + vibe-coding-native poles
- **#5 (lightning Z) + #10 (custom wordmark)** - both icon-only mark + wordmark, broad usable system
- **#7 (esports badge) + #9 (hand-drawn)** - league/permanence vs indie/realness, see which the brand wants to be

## Constraints to flag

- **NO emojis or em dashes** anywhere in brand assets (Zaal's hard rule across the whole ZAO ecosystem)
- **The joystick = A issue** in the current draft - any letter substitution must be unambiguous
- **Must NOT use "ZIBAL"** - common misread; designer should test the wordmark on 5 strangers to confirm they read ZABAL
- **Season-swappable element** - Season 1 / Season 2 / Season 3 should reuse the same mark with a single changing piece (numeral, ribbon, color shift)
- **Works on dark bg by default** - site is `#070709`. Light-bg variant for sponsor decks needed.

## What we want back

- 2-3 mockups (whichever directions the designer is most excited about)
- Each mockup rendered at 1024x1024 + 1200x630
- One rationale paragraph per concept (why this direction won the internal vote)
- Open to a fourth wildcard direction we did not list
