Today's mindful moment: Become a Better Listener.

From Don't Sweat the Small Stuff by Richard Carlson. Carlson's point is that most of us are so busy waiting for our turn to talk that we stop actually hearing the other person - and the moment we slow down and truly listen, both the conversation and the relationship get better.

For builders, this is the whole point of an AMA. The sharpest question in the room is worth more than the answer you already had ready. Listen first - to your users, to the chat, to the question behind the question - then build. The best builds start as someone else's question.
