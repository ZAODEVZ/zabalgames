## The fireside angle: the mentality of Farcaster builders

The booked topic is the culture, not the product. Jub Jub has a rare vantage point - he watches a
whole cohort of builders present every batch - so the conversation can go at what makes Farcaster
builders tick: why they ship in public, what "build the thing you want to see" looks like in
practice, what separates the builds that stick from the ones that fade, and how a showcase like
Batches changes how people build. His own work (JubJub, tokenizing media ownership + creator
payments) is a fine thread on where the creator economy is heading, if it comes up.

## Suggested running order (Space fireside, ~45-60 min)

1. **Who Jub Jub is + why he started Batches** (~8 min) - the origin and the goal of the showcase.
2. **What he sees across a cohort** (~12 min) - patterns in how Farcaster builders work, the
   "build the thing you want to see" mentality, what surprises him.

   builds from one-week wonders. (Natural bridge to the ZABAL Gamez July build.)

   how to present and ship well.
