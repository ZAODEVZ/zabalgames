## Outline

**1. Cold open - the moment that proves it (2 min)**
- A musician earned ~0.5 SOL in about 20 minutes of battle activity alone. Not streams - attention.
- Thesis: artists earn from engagement, not just plays.

**2. What WaveWarZ is, in plain terms (4 min)**
- Head-to-head music battles. Your track goes up against another artist's; people back who they think wins.
- You earn a cut of all that activity on your battles, and it keeps flowing to you.
- You keep your masters, your IP, your audience. WaveWarZ never holds any of it.
- Live now: hundreds of battles, 40+ artists, 400+ SOL of activity (May 2026 snapshot).
- For the curious (10 sec): it runs on Solana today, settles by smart contract, no custody.

**3. What it actually gives an artist (6 min)**
- A new income stream tied to attention, on top of streaming.
- A recurring audience: ~11 live shows/week (5 morning + 5 night Mon-Fri, plus Sunday battles) and ~11 live Spaces/week.
- Competition that drives discovery - new ears every battle.
- A leaderboard to climb (early leaders: LUI, APORKALYPSE, PROF!T, Kata7yst).

**4. Live walkthrough (6 min)**
- Screen-share `wavewarz-intelligence.vercel.app`: a real battle, the activity, how earnings build.

**5. How an artist gets in (4 min)**
- How a battle works start to finish, and the weekly schedule.
- How to promote your own battle so the activity (and your cut) grows.
- A tip or two from the artists already winning.

**6. Where it's headed + why it matters this season (3 min)**
- v2 is being built on Base (Sam + Arthur) - same idea, ZAO's home rails.
- It plugs into ZABAL Gamez: the August Finals settle through WaveWarZ-Base, and the Finals are its launch moment.

**7. Three things to do next (3 min)**
1. Follow @WaveWarZ and drop into a Space.
2. Learn more - watch a few battles on the live site, get a feel for it.
3. Start thinking about what you could build on top of WaveWarZ - the bridge into the July build-a-thon. (Hand to the builder track.)

**Attendee walks away with:** how a battle earns them money, the weekly schedule, the live link, and three concrete next steps.
