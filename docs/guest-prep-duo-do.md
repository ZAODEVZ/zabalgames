## Who Duo Do is (best current read - confirm)

A genre-fluid music duo on Farcaster (@duodomusica, also on X at x.com/duodomusica and Facebook).
Self-description: music without genre, in movement. Spanish-language / Latin context (e.g. a live
show "La Sonoridad Oculta" at a cultural center). This is the artist-track angle: an independent
act using Farcaster as a direct channel to listeners.

The two halves of the talk:
1. **Musician on Farcaster** - releasing and growing as an independent act on Farcaster: finding
   listeners, turning them into a community, owning the relationship instead of renting reach.
2. **The road to Farcon** - using Farcaster momentum to build toward Farcon (the Farcaster
   conference): why an in-person moment matters for an online music act and how to make it count.
