# The ZAO -> ZABAL Gamez - presentation talking-track

A speaker outline for presenting the ZAO ecosystem and ZABAL Gamez live. Open by
playing Iman's clip out loud, then walk the four beats: what The ZAO is, how it works,
what it has produced, and how ZABAL Gamez brings it all together. Bullets are talking
points to riff on, not a word-for-word script. Target ~6-8 minutes.

North star (open or close with this): The ZAO exists to bring the profit margins, the
data, and the IP rights back to independent artists. ZABAL Gamez is how we build the
tools to do it, together, in public.

---

## 0. Open - play Iman's clip (~53s)
- Cue the audio first. Let it set the room before you say anything.
- One-line handoff after it ends: "That's ZABAL Gamez. Let me back up and show you where it comes from and why it matters."

## 1. What The ZAO is (~1 min)
- The ZAO is an impact network for the creator economy.
- Our goal is simple: bring the profit margins, the data, and the IP rights back to independent artists.
- Today it is a 100+ member music community. Founded in 2022. Community first, technology second.
- The line to land: "We are not here to teach you crypto. We are here to help you own more of what you already make."

## 2. How The ZAO works (~1.5 min)
- The ZAO is an incubator that runs on social capital.
- You bring an idea. You assemble the best possible partners around it, based on who is actually willing to show up and support it. The support comes first; the tools and the token come second.
- Concrete proof of the method: in our very first workshop this month, yerbearserker walked the room through the Triple-A framework - Assemble, Affirm, Ascend. Assemble the people and the tools first. The token is the amplifier, not the foundation.
- The line to land: "Your idea earns its partners. If people show up to support it, you build it. That is the whole engine."

## 3. What that engine has produced (~2.5 min)
Walk each one so you hit every part. One or two sentences each is enough.

1. WaveWarZ - music-battle prediction markets. Artists go head to head, the audience trades on the outcome, and the artist earns from that activity, not just from streams. Hundreds of battles, 40+ artists, 400+ SOL traded. A v2 is in build on Base.
2. ZAO Festivals - the IRL arm. ZAOstock is the first one: October 3rd in Ellsworth, Maine. A full day of artists, livestreamed, with an after-party. A real festival first, the tech enhances it.
3. ZAO Fractals - how reputation is earned. Small peer groups rank each other's contribution every week. It has run 100+ weeks straight, and it is what powers governance.
4. The ZAO newsletter - the community's written signal. How we keep everyone caught up on what is shipping.
5. The podcasts - three shows: Let's Talk About Web Three, Let's Talk About ETH, and BCZ YapZ, the long-form interview show.
6. The collaborations - we build with other communities too: Midi-ZAO-NKZ, and COC Concertz, the virtual-concert community running streamed shows.
- The line to land: "None of these came from a roadmap. Each one came from a member with an idea and enough people willing to back it."

## 4. ZABAL Gamez brings it all together (~1.5 min)
- ZABAL Gamez is a three-month build event that ties the whole ecosystem together. It is not a video-game contest - it is how we build the next round of tools, in public.
- The shape:
  - June - workshop month. Members share short sessions on the tools they have built, so you can learn them first-hand.
  - July - open build. You pick an idea and ship it for the ecosystem. The build is the application.
  - August - the Finals. The strongest builds get a ZAO mentor as a teammate, and a live reveal.
- It is free, open to anyone, and you can use any tools you like. Three tracks: artist, builder, creator - there is a lane for everyone.
- Every finalist who ships gets paid and keeps the ongoing economics of what they make. There is a $500 prize pool to start, and we are growing it.
- How to get in: start at zabalgamez.com, and collect the connector on Magnetiq to register.
- The line to close: "Bring an idea. We will help you find the people to build it. Insert coin."

## 5. Close + where to go
- Recap in one breath: impact network -> incubator on social capital -> the projects it produced -> ZABAL Gamez to build the next ones.
- Point them at: zabalgamez.com, the /zabal group chat, and the Luma calendar for the live workshops.
- Invitation to leave them with: "If you have an idea for the creator economy, this is the summer to build it. Come find us."
