## 1. What The ZAO is (~40s)
- The ZAO is an impact network for the creator economy.
- The goal: bring the profit margins, the data, and the IP rights back to independent artists.
- Today: a 100+ member music community, since 2022. Community first, technology second.
- Land it: "We are not here to teach you crypto. We help you own more of what you already make."

## 2. How it works (~40s)
- The ZAO is an incubator that runs on social capital. You bring an idea; you assemble partners around it based on who actually shows up to support it. Support first, tools and token second.
- (If time) yerbearserker's Triple-A from our first workshop: Assemble, Affirm, Ascend. The token is the amplifier, not the foundation.
- Land it: "Your idea earns its partners. That is the whole engine."

## 3. Play Iman's clip (~53s) - the bridge
- Set it up in one line: "So what do we do with all of that? Here's Iman."
- Cue the audio. Let it land.
- Handoff out: "That's ZABAL Gamez - and here is what we are actually bringing together."

## 4. ZABAL Gamez - bringing it together (~3:05)
The core message: this build-a-thon puts every project the ZAO has incubated in one place, so anyone can build on top of the ecosystem and actually understand it.

First, name what we have incubated (one sentence each, resist the riff):
1. WaveWarZ - music-battle markets. Artists earn from the audience trading on their battles, not just streams. 40+ artists, 400+ SOL traded; v2 in build on Base.
2. ZAO Festivals - the IRL arm. ZAOstock is the first: October 3rd, Ellsworth Maine. A real festival, livestreamed.
3. ZAO Fractals - how reputation is earned. Peer groups rank each other's contribution weekly, 100+ weeks running. It powers governance.
4. The ZAO newsletter - how the community stays caught up on what is shipping.
5. The podcasts - Let's Talk About Web Three, Let's Talk About ETH, and BCZ YapZ.
6. The collabs - we build with other communities too: Midi-ZAO-NKZ and COC Concertz.

Then the build-a-thon itself:
- ZABAL Gamez brings all of those together for one three-month build-a-thon, so anyone can build on top of the ecosystem and understand it more. Not a video-game contest - it is how we open the doors.
- June: workshop month, members teach the tools they built. July: open build, the build is the application. August: Finals, each strong build gets a ZAO mentor as a teammate.
- Free, open to anyone, any tools. Three tracks: artist, builder, creator.
- Every finalist who ships gets paid and keeps the ongoing economics. $500 pool to start, and growing.
- Get in: zabalgamez.com, and collect the connector on Magnetiq to register.
- Land it: "All of it, in one place, open to build on. That is ZABAL Gamez."

## Q&A prep (the ~4:00 after) - have these ready
- "Name an artist who keeps more margin/IP because of The ZAO." -> WaveWarZ: a musician earned ~0.5 SOL in 20 minutes of trade activity in one early battle. Plus ZAO Music rails: DistroKid 100% royalty retention + 0xSplits paying out on-chain, no label tax. Concrete, not vision.
- "Why crypto at all?" -> The rails just remove middlemen (publishing, splits, custody). Community first, tech second - a member never has to touch a wallet to benefit.
- "Isn't WaveWarZ just gambling on artists?" -> 1% of trade volume flows to the artist, settled on-chain, no custody. Artists earn from engagement, not only streams. The point is paying artists for attention.
- "$500 for three months?" -> Getting selected is the first win: every finalist who ships gets paid, keeps the ongoing economics, and pairs with a mentor. The pool is v0 and growing; we are looking for sponsors (info@thezao.com).
- "Why should anyone outside your 100 members care?" -> The strongest builds are aimed beyond insiders - if it only works for people who already know us, it does not place. Composability is the pull.
- "What if Zaal steps away?" -> Governance is earned and distributed through Fractals/Respect, not one person; the projects are member-owned and open-source (MIT).
