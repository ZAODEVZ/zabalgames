# Media distribution - the go-live runbook + channel registry

> How a post actually reaches people when we go live, and every channel we can push to. Pairs
> with `docs/media-playbook.md` (which defines the post formats). Brand rules apply everywhere:
> no emojis, no em dashes (hyphens only), no crypto/web3 jargon, "100+" for member count.

## The go-live runbook (run this the moment you press Live)
1. **Firefly** - post the live-now post. Firefly fans it out to X, Farcaster, Lens, and Bluesky
   in one action.
2. **The 50-second intro video** - post it as the intro plays. It buys you the window to do the rest.
3. In that window, drop the live-now post into the manual channels:
   - **Discord** - [server / channel]
   - **Telegram** - [group / channel]
   - **Farcaster group chat - ZABAL** - [link]
   - **Farcaster group chat - ZAO** - [link]
   - **ZAO X group chat** - [link]
4. Pin the post where pinning helps (Discord, Telegram, the GCs).

The idea: Firefly covers the public feeds in one shot; the 50-second intro is the timer; the
group chats are the warm, high-intent audiences you hit by hand during that timer.
