# Workshop topics by track - ZABAL Gamez Season 1

Idea bank for June workshop sessions. Use it two ways: (1) when pitching a specific
person, point them at the bucket they fit; (2) for anyone who signs up unsure what to
teach, this gives them a starting list. Each is a ~30-minute session, livestreamed and
recorded. Topics are open - this is a prompt, not a fence.

Three tracks: **artist** (musical/visual), **builder** (developer/aspiring),
**creator** (media/distribution). Many people fit more than one - pick the angle.

---

## ARTIST - musical or visual

For musicians, producers, visual artists, designers. "Here is how I make the thing,
and how it can live in this ecosystem."

- Making music in the ZAO ecosystem - your production workflow, start to finish
- Releasing a track or EP to a Farcaster audience - what worked, what didn't
- Visual identity + brand art - building a look (the arcade/pixel direction is one example)
- AI-assisted art workflows - tools, prompts, getting a usable result
- Generative + collectible art - from idea to a mintable piece
- Sound design + sample creation - making the raw material
- Cover art, motion, and music video on a budget
- Performing + live sets - COC Concertz and live formats
- Songjam - where music meets the social graph
- WaveWarZ from the artist side - your music as the thing people back

## BUILDER - developer or aspiring

For coders, vibe-coders, and no-code builders. "Here is a tool/rail and how to ship
with it." (Confirmed leads live here so far - good company.)

- Empire Builder V3 - leaderboards, Empires, token mechanics (yerbearzerker)
- Magnetiq - the workshop-library platform itself (Tyler)
- Launching a token with Clanker - the SDK, fees, airdrops
- Build a Farcaster Mini App start to finish - manifest, embed, one tap to live
- Remix / Snaps - shippable, graduatable games
- Vibe-coding with Claude Code / Cursor - agent harnesses, MCP, the loop that works
- Quick Auth + Sign In With Farcaster - verified identity, server-side
- Building onchain on Base - Hats, EAS attestations, 0xSplits payouts
- WaveWarZ-Base - the contracts and the Finals settlement surface
- Bonfire - the ZAO memory/knowledge layer and how to read/write it
- POIDH bounties - build something and get paid for it
- zlank.online templates - one-click casts and reusable surfaces

## CREATOR - media and distribution

For video, writing, streaming, growth, and community people. "Here is how you get a
thing seen and keep people coming back."

- Going viral on Farcaster - the in-feed loop, tagging, what actually spreads
- Running an interview show - format, prep, turning it into a series (BCZ YapZ)
- Short-form clipping - turning one session into ten pieces
- Long-form on Paragraph - newsletters and essays that land
- Community + channel building - starting and growing a /channel
- Live streaming - Retake.tv and live formats
- Podcasting - the GM Farcaster playbook
- Growth loops - referrals, streaks, notifications, the mechanics that retain
- Cross-platform distribution - moving one idea across Farcaster, X, Telegram
- Turning a build into content - demos, threads, docs that sell the work

## How to use this for outreach

- Match a person to a bucket, then pitch the single most specific topic they'd own.
- A confirmed lead's exact topic goes into `data/workshop-leads.json` (drives the live
  schedule) once locked. This file is the idea bank; the roster is in
  `docs/workshop-roster.md`.
