Today's card: Make Peace with Imperfection.

From Don't Sweat the Small Stuff by Richard Carlson. The pursuit of perfect keeps you focused on what is wrong - with your work, with yourself - and pulls you out of the moment you are actually in. Notice when the gap between what is and what should be is running the show, and gently let it go. Things do not have to be perfect to be good, or to be shipped.

For builders, this is the whole game on a learning day. The first version is allowed to be rough. Open the tool, build the imperfect thing, put it in front of someone, and let the next version be better than the last. Done and shared beats perfect and hidden, every time. The build is the application.
