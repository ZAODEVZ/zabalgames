## The 3-minute talk

**Hook.** ZABAL Gamez is The ZAO's three-month build event. Not a video-game contest - a build-a-thon for the Farcaster and ZAO ecosystem. The build is the application.

**The shape.** Three months, three phases. June is workshop month - builders teach what they have shipped. July is the open build - anyone ships something real for the ecosystem, in public. August is the Finals. Free, open to anyone, any harness, three tracks: artist, builder, creator.

**Why it is different.** You are not building a throwaway demo. You build on live ZAO rails - Empire Builder, Bonfire, Farcaster - for a 100+ member community that actually needs the tools, with a ZAO mentor as your teammate. Every finisher shares the prize pool, keeps the repo, and keeps the upside.

**Proof, week one.** We hit Start and kept the receipts. yerbearserker opened on Empire Builder and stood up a tokenless Empire live. Joshua and Plat0x walked Bonfire, the shared memory the whole thing runs on. Ohnahji opened the creator track. We spent the week on GM Farcaster's Farcaster Batches with a dozen builders, and closed with a vibe-coding fireside. Every session is recorded, transcribed, and kept - for people and for agents.

**The ask.** Two doors. If you have shipped something worth teaching, lead a June workshop. If you want to build, July is open - there are 60+ started projects waiting to be adopted, and the build is the application. Everything is at zabalgamez.com.

**Close.** One week down. Insert Coin.
