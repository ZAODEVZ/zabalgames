# ZABAL Gamez - Validation Launch Kit

> Date: 2026-05-29. Purpose: run the FounderCheck VALIDATE experiment - 10 June
> workshop signups in 72 hours from builders outside the immediate circle. Pulls
> from `docs/positioning-2026-05-29.md`. Voice: `docs/brand-kit-2026-05-28.md`.

## The experiment

- Target: 10 workshop signups in 72h, from outside the immediate circle.
- Levers: one /zabal announce cast + 10 personal DMs to FarHack and Farcaster
  Agentic Bootcamp alumni + 1 ZAO core endorsement cast.
- Read the result honestly: 10+ from outside = demand is real, push harder. 5-9 =
  promising, tighten the offer. Under 5 = the offer or the audience is off; fix
  before scaling spend. The score is not the validation - the signups are.

### What counts as "outside the immediate circle"

Decide this before you post, not after, or the test grades itself generously.

- COUNTS (cold-ish): someone you have not built with, not a current ZAO member, found
  you via the /zabal cast, a reshare, or a cold-ish DM. FarHack / Agentic Bootcamp
  alumni you have not worked with count.
- DOES NOT count toward the 10: current ZAO members, people you have shipped with,
  close friends, anyone you would have texted anyway. Log them, but in a separate
  "warm" tally.

Target is 10 from the COUNTS column. If 8 of your signups are warm, that is real
enthusiasm worth noting - but it validates your circle, not external demand. Per the
advisor: hit 10+ from outside and the offer has proven it resonates when framed right
(Ease of sale 5 -> 6).

## 4. Objection handlers (preempt the FounderCheck gaps)

Keep these ready - drop them into replies, not all at once.

- "3 months is a big commitment." -> You pick one door. July's ship is the only real
  ask, and that can be a weekend. June workshops are watch-anytime. August Finals is
  opt-in and only for selected builds. Three doors, pick yours.
- "Is $500 worth it?" -> The money is the bonus, not the draw. You get a ZAO mentor as
  a teammate, Respect (real governance weight), the 1% WaveWarZ trade cut that keeps
  flowing to you after the Finals, ZAO distribution behind your ship, and a repo you
  keep. Every finisher gets paid and a Hats NFT.
- "Will the tools I build actually get used?" -> You build on live ZAO rails for a
  100+ member community that needs them - not a throwaway demo. Real surface, real users.
