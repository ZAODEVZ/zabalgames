## What a fractal is (the one-paragraph version)

A fractal is a small-group consensus meeting that distributes a soulbound reputation token
called **Respect**. The community meets on a regular cadence; people split into randomized
groups of three to six, each person gets up to four minutes to share their recent
contributions, then the group ranks those contributions by helpfulness and, once there is
at least two-thirds consensus, everyone in the group earns Respect. Governance weight comes
from actual contribution, not from how much capital you hold. That meeting is the **Respect
Game**.

## The history (the EOS story), in order

1. **Lessons from BitShares + Steem/Hive (2014-2020).** Daniel Larimer builds three
   blockchains and learns the failure modes of token-weighted voting (whales capture
   governance). The fractal idea is the fix.
2. **"More Equal Animals: The Subtle Art of True Democracy" (Feb 2021).** Larimer's book
   lays out the theory of decentralized governance that fractals implement.
3. **Eden on EOS (2021).** The first live test - a community of hundreds running fractal
   democracy via "Eden Elections," three elections held in 2021. Deemed a success and the
   direct inspiration for what comes next.
4. **fractally white paper (Feb 22 2022, 22:22 UTC).** "Introducing Fractally - the next
   generation of DAOs" (goƒractally). Formalizes the weekly Respect Game + the Respect
   token, framed as delivering the original 2017 EOS vision.
5. **Genesis Fractal (2022).** A ~30-week experiment with about 130 participants
   (avg ~37 per meeting) that pioneered the fractally process in practice.
6. **Eden Fractal (founded May 2022).** Respected Genesis Fractal members continued the
   process inside the Eden community. Has met weekly ever since (avg ~15 participants).
   Now in **Epoch 2**: moving from experimentation to implementation - deploying ORDAO on
   Base, running the Respect Game, a democratic consensus process, plus a Fractal Fellowship
   and a Fractal Development Fund. (Recent cadence: Thursdays 17 UTC.)
7. **Optimism Fractal (Oct 2023, by the Optimystics team).** Brought the model to the
   Ethereum / Optimism Superchain - a council formed with Respect as a soulbound reputation
   token earned by helping Optimism. Optimystics also build the tooling (Respect Game app,
   Fractalgram).
8. **ZAO Fractal (Aug 2024).** The ZAO's implementation, and the **first music-focused
   fractal**. Weekly Respect Game, Mondays 6pm EST, an unbroken 100+ week streak. Soulbound
   **ZAO Respect** earned through peer consensus, four objectives (empowerment, transparency,
   sustainability, equitable distribution), smart contracts, and a Discord bot.

## The ecosystem map (today)

- **Eden Fractal** - the longest-running community fractal, now Epoch 2 on Base.
- **Optimism Fractal / Optimystics** - the Superchain implementation + the tooling team
  (Respect Game app, Fractalgram, the fractal-history writeups).
- **ZAO Fractal** - the music/creator fractal, The ZAO's governance layer.
- Shared DNA across all of them: the Respect Game, soulbound Respect, contribution-weighted
  governance, the fractally lineage.
