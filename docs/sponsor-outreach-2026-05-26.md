# Sponsor Outreach - Template + Pitch

> For anyone Zaal wants to ask about v1 prize-pool funding or v0 supplemental support. Replies go to info@thezao.com (ZAO Festivals team handles the books).

## Quick context for cold receivers

Zaal sends. ZAO Festivals team owns the sponsor relationship + invoicing once they reply.

The $500 v0 pool from The ZAO Festivals team is the floor. Anything additional goes into v1 expansion or v0 specific supports (mentor coffee, OG card design, livestream production, prize-pool top-up).

## Letter template (copy-paste, adjust the bracketed bits)

```

Hey [Name],

Quick context - ZABAL Games is The ZAO's 3-month Build-A-Thon for digital
creators in the Farcaster ecosystem. June workshops, July open build month,
August Finals with 8 builders picking champions for a 5-day paired build
window.

The v0 (Season 1) prize pool is $500 USDC sponsored by The ZAO Festivals
team. Top 8 builders share USDC, Top 16 get $ZABAL token rewards on top.

We are looking for sponsors who want to:
- Grow the v1 pool (Season 2 onward)

  designer + OG card work, anything else that helps shipped)

Why this might be a fit for [you/your team]:
[1-2 lines of why - their audience, their values, their stack alignment]

Reply to info@thezao.com (the ZAO Festivals team handles sponsor
inquiries + invoicing) or DM me directly if you want to talk specifics.

Site: zabalgames.com
Public log: zabalgames.com/changelog

Thanks for taking a look,
Zaal
@bettercallzaal
```

## How to position the ask

**Don't:**
- Promise sponsor benefits beyond "attribution + thank-you on site + Finals stream credit"
- Quote specific dollar tiers ("for $1000 you get X") - we don't have a tier rate card built yet
- Make it sound like a media buy - it's a sponsorship of an open ecosystem event

**Do:**
- Frame as "support an open ecosystem build month"
- Point to the public log + the workshop schedule
- Make replies go to info@thezao.com to keep the relationship with the right entity

## After they reply

1. Forward the reply to info@thezao.com if it came to your inbox
2. ZAO Festivals team responds with specifics + invoicing
3. Once confirmed, add to the changelog ("[Sponsor] joins v1 prize pool")
4. Update the site sponsor card from "The ZAO festivals team" to "[Sponsor] x The ZAO festivals team" or expand the card to multiple sponsors
5. Cast a thank-you on /zabal + cross-post

## What you DON'T have authority to commit to

Per the no-fabrication rule, do not promise:
- Specific dollar amounts they'll receive back
- Tax-deductibility status
- ROI metrics (impressions, click-throughs)
- Specific co-branded content beyond "thank-you mention on site + Finals stream"
- Exclusivity in any category
- Timeline for v1 (Season 2) launch

These all sit with The ZAO Festivals team to commit to once they're in the conversation.

## What's true that you can promise

Honest statements you can make:

- "The site is at zabalgames.com - we will list your name + link on the sponsor card"
- "Your name + thank-you will land on the Finals livestream credits"
- "You will get cast attribution on /zabal channel when v1 announcement goes out"
- "If you want a custom integration (booth at a future ZAO event, etc.), we can talk that through ZAO Festivals separately"
