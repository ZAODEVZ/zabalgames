## What Magnetiq is (the one-paragraph version)

Magnetiq is a community-marketing platform for brands and creators: instead of renting reach
on social platforms, you siphon your audience into a space you own and keep, and turn customers
into ambassadors. It runs tiered status and exclusive access, event activations (flash drops,
UGC challenges, time-bound campaigns), post-purchase product extensions, and VIP/loyalty by
recognition rather than points, with analytics that tie participation and behavior to purchase.
For ZABAL Gamez specifically, Magnetiq hosts the season's workshop/vendor library plus entry
and onboarding in one portal, and the season collectible (the "magnet").

## The methodology: SNAPS

Tyler's retention frame, the spine of the pitch. Members feel ownership ("emotional loyalty")
when you hit some combination of:

- **S**tatus - standing in the community.
- **N**ovelty - something new to come back for.
- **A**ccess - to people, drops, or spaces others do not have.
- **P**ower - a say, influence, co-creation.
- **S**tuff - tangible perks and rewards.

His sharpest argument: "Anyone can hit a viral post in 2026 ... but what does that actually do
for your bottom line?" The content is the easy part; getting people in and keeping them is the
work. And: "You're building on rented land and the rent keeps going up."

This is the bridge from Tyler's pitch into what the ZABAL Gamez audience is already standing on.

- **ZABAL Gamez runs on Magnetiq.** The workshop/vendor library plus entry and onboarding are
  hosted in one Magnetiq portal. The live ZABAL surface: `app.magnetiq.xyz/brand/zabal`.
- **The season collectible / magnet:** `app.magnetiq.xyz/brand/zabal/magnet/zabal-gamez` - the
  registration + brand-mementos + UGC-upload surface for the season.
- **The "Zabal connector" anchor magnet.** The pattern from the call: one connector magnet acts
  like a Discord "general" channel - collecting it auto-drops the downstream entry (e.g. the
  road-to-ZAOstock entry), and other magnets are the side channels. A clean live demo of how a
  builder would structure their own community.
- **Opt-in data angle.** Tyler frames opt-in as "the new currency of the internet" - every click
  in a mini app is visible to the client, so the ZAO/Magnetiq move is to lead by example on
  opt-in data (Magnetiq even offered to build YouTube watch-analytics / drop-off for ZAO). A good
  builder-track talking point if the room is technical.
