# Workshop 1 recap - Empire Builder: From Dream to Empire

**Date:** 2026-06-04 (morning), first official ZABAL Gamez workshop, recorded.
**Host:** BetterCallZaal (Zaal). **Guest:** yerbearserker / Jordan - co-founder and
chief ecosystem architect, Empire Builder. **Also present:** Adrian (Diviflyy),
co-founder and chief technology architect (watching); live audience Q and A.

Video (unlisted, share with yerbearserker first): title "Don't Launch a Token Yet -
From Dream to Empire".

## Summary
Jordan walked through Empire Builder's thesis: do not launch a token until you are
ready. Build a real project and community first, then let the token amplify it. He
framed the Empire V3 no-code tool stack and the Triple A path (Assemble, Affirm,
Ascend). He and Zaal agreed to spin up a tokenless ZABAL Gamez empire for the season.

## Key points
- Sequence matters. The token is the amplifier, not the foundation. Most launches go
  token-first, spike, get extracted, and collapse.
- Values: generative not extractive, symbiotic not parasitic. A throughline from day
  one, not a pivot.
- Empire V3 tool stack: treasury (Splits audited contracts), multi-chain (Base,
  Arbitrum), leaderboards, boosters, distribution (airdrops, USDC, NFTs, raffles),
  staking (boosts leaderboard standing rather than paying passive yield, to avoid
  looking like a security), and AI skills (connect your own model).
- Three tiers: Assembled (tokenless, full tool stack), Awakened, Ascended (full suite,
  around 40 boosters, launch the token through Empire).
- Triple A framework: Assemble (build the harness, no token) - Affirm (tactical
  generosity: reward your active people, including via Farcaster social-graph
  leaderboards) - Ascend (launch the token as an event once the ecosystem can carry it).
- More than a cookie: reach and reward specific real holders and individuals, not
  Web2 cookie cohorts. Reward with intention, not spray and pray.
- Support: priority chat for launches, group chat, advisory and consulting, technical
  writing from Adrian.
- Credibility: 5,761+ empires created, two part-time founders, zero external funding.
  Empire V3 launched at FARCON Rome. Partnered with Clanker.
