## Description
```
You are not beholden to the algorithm. You can build your own lens. Chris (chriscocreated), founder of Sopha, walks through the Farcaster client he built for depth and high signal - and why curation is the whole point. Find Sopha: sopha.social

The feed is ephemeral - we go there, we shitpost, and the deep thinking scrolls right past. Sopha is an intentional third place - a coffee shop, a library, a cultural center - for the high-signal writing, art, and thinking you would otherwise miss. In this ZABAL Gamez workshop, Chris traces why he built it (when Farcaster felt like it was tipping into meme coins, he set out to protect the culture), how curation actually works on Sopha, and how genuinely easy it now is to build your own client on Farcaster's open data.

What you walk away with:
- Curation is the point - a client built for depth, not a replacement for the daily feed
- Building your own Farcaster client is now a few hours of work, with open data plus Neynar (and Hypersnap)
- You are not beholden to the algorithm - you build your own lens, the unlock for artists, writers, and creators
- The nested stack: a client is just a website showing Farcaster data, with mini apps below it, and Snaps you can spin up in seconds
- How Sopha's curated feed works - community curators who earn the role with taste, plus BradQ's The Pulse
- Where Sopha is headed: a second Purple round, rewarding curators, infra, live cultural-center experiences, and a token only if it stays non-extractive
```
