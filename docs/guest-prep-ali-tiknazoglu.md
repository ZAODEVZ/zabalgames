## What "previbecoding" is (best read - confirm with Ali)

"Previbecoding" is Ali's own term; it is not a web-indexed concept, so treat the definition as
his to give. From the booked topic, the best read: the on-ramp **before** vibe-coding - what a
true beginner needs to learn and set up to build well with AI tooling, rather than just prompting
blindly. The audience is explicitly "people who are learning to code now," and the angle is "what
he is learning" as a working builder, not a polished curriculum. Get his one-line definition at
the top of the session so the framing is his, not ours.

He shipped a real product (Inflynce) as a Base/Farcaster miniapp while learning - so the talk is
