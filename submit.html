Submission questions

Does my project need to be finished?

No. Choose “Work in progress” and update it later. Clear progress is more useful than waiting for a perfect launch.

Do I need a wallet or Farcaster account?

No. Anyone can submit. Identity or payout information is collected only when it is needed for prizes, voting, or collectibles.

Do I need a GitHub repository?

No. Repositories are useful for software projects, but songs, visuals, videos, writing, events, and other creator work can use the media or demo field instead.

What happens after I submit?

Your project enters a private review queue. The ZAO may request more information. Once approved, the public project record can appear across ZABAL Gamez.

Want to answer the season survey?

The project form is for things you make. Feedback, wins, contact preferences, and the collectible prompts now live in the separate Co-Lab.
