Start here.

ZABAL Gamez is The ZAO's 3-month build event - free, open to anyone, and live right now. You do not need a plan or a finished anything. Pick your lane, take three small actions, and you are in the season.

1. Pick your lane

Tap one - it remembers, and in the Farcaster app it counts you in.

2. Three small actions

1

Play one game

The arcade deals you two, pick one. Every July board is fresh - your first score can take a top spot.

 2 min -> 2

Post one thing as a WIP

An idea, a half-build, a win from the first half of the year. Not finished is fine - drafts are public and people comment while you work.

Say ZM in the group chat

100+ people building the same season. One ZM and you are known.
