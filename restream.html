Going live on Restream

ZABAL Gamez workshops stream on Restream. This is everything a guest or streamer needs - join from your browser, connect your channels, watch the chat before you go on, and look great on camera.

Joining as a guest? The 60-second version

  1. Open the invite link the host sends you - in a browser, on a laptop. No account, no download.
  2. Allow camera and mic, type your name, and click Join.
  3. You land backstage - you can see and hear the stream and read the chat, but you are not on air yet.
  4. The host brings you on when it is your turn. Headphones on, good light, wired internet if you can.
