How the Finals Decide

WaveWarZ-Base market + Respect governance

Each Finalist becomes a tradable battle entry on WaveWarZ-Base. The market trades for 72h on real signal (live demo + repo + cast quality). Final ranking = market price weighted with judge-agent rubric scores. Live settlement readout fires at T+72h on /finals/live.

Respect (soulbound, non-transferable governance signal) has a voice in the settlement. The specific Respect-weight mechanism is being designed with the WaveWarZ-Base contracts and locked before the August Finals.

About Respect

Respect is illiquid. Cannot be sold, transferred, or traded. It is The ZAO's governance signal - you earn it by participating in the ecosystem.

During ZABAL Gamez: up to 1000 Respect points earnable per active participant. Current ZAO holders determine who counts as active (workshop attendance, July submissions, August Finals participation, ecosystem contribution).

Voter eligibility: 1000+ Respect points = eligible to vote in Finals settlement. Threshold may shift as the event unfolds. The 1000+ cohort can also vote (66%+ majority) to change the governance system itself.
