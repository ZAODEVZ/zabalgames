How the Finals Decide

WaveWarZ-Base hybrid voting (Doc 720)

Each Finalist becomes a tradable battle entry. Respect-holders get pre-funded positions (~$1-2 USDC each). The market trades for 72h. Final ranking = market price weighted with judge-agent rubric scores. Live settlement readout fires at T+72h on /finals/live.

Why this and not Respect 1p1v? Because price discovery + skin-in-the-game beats pure popularity vote for "which build will actually get used." The Respect-holder baseline keeps the floor honest for builds that are valuable-but-niche.
