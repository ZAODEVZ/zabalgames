Welcome to the ZAO Fractal. I'm teaching our next ZAO Gamez workshop, and we're talking about the ZAO Fractal as the first ZAO project today, since we're in our ZAO Fractal meeting. We meet weekly at a regular time, 6pm Eastern, in the ZAO Fractal at discord.theZAO.com.

At the ZAO, we are a decentralized impact network for the creator economy. What is the ZAO? The ZAO is the Z Talent Artist Organization, powered by soulbound, illiquid ZAO Respect tokens that you can earn weekly through peer consensus. We have four objectives: empowerment, transparency, sustainability, and equitable distribution.

We're here to play the ZAO Respect game. How does it work? We split off into groups of a maximum of six people and share four-minute updates - if you're new, an intro; if you're returning, what you've been contributing since your last ZAO Fractal. These are all submitted on chain at the end.

What does voting mean? What are you contributing? Anything you think is advancing the ZAO's vision of advancing music, art, and technology - in one of five ways: contributions, collaborations, innovations, onboarding, and supporting ZAO community members. Supporting community members is any way, shape, or form. Contributions start with anything you're working on - as a developer, an artist, an entrepreneur - anything you believe advances the long-term goal. The extra includes collaborations (working with anyone out there), innovations (anything cutting edge), and onboarding (bringing people into the ZAO, into on-chain music, or into crypto as a whole).

We use Discord, with two tools. The dash timer tool gets everyone started and drops a "meet the group" embed in the chat so you can see who else is in your group. It shows who is currently speaking, and you choose who goes next - and if someone needs to be brought back, you can always return to them. After everyone shares, we reach consensus. The facilitator creates the consensus bot with the dash ZAO Fractal command, and everyone votes on the level-six person - the one you believe contributed most this week - then level five, four, three, two, one, until everyone has a level. After that, you get the ZAO Fractal results.

We're currently in the ZAO Fractal meeting, so if you turn on your camera, you can earn an additional ten ZAO Respect. Does anyone have any questions before we get started? Awesome. Thank you.
