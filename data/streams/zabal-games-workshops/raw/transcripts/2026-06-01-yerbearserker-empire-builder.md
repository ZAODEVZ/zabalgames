## Key points

- Core message: **do not launch a token until you are ready.** You get one chance at a first
  impression, and most token launchers are extractive.
- An Empire is not a token. It is the harness - treasury, leaderboards, boosters, staking,
  distribution, AI skills - that coordinates your energy and your community.
- **Triple-A framework:** Assemble (build the tokenless harness, find your people), Affirm
  (reward and activate them with tactical generosity), Ascend (launch a token, intentionally,
  as the amplifier once the ecosystem can carry it).
- **Three tiers:** Assembled (tokenless), Awakened (attach a token tool stack to an existing
  token), Ascended (full stack, launched through Empire Builder).
- Generative not extractive, symbiotic not parasitic - the throughline from day one.
- Over 5,761 Empires created, built by two part-time founders with zero external funding.
- Live result: a tokenless **ZABAL Gamez Empire** was created during the session.
