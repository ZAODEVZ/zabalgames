## Key points

- **DEKEY** (Node): a peer-to-peer encrypted file marketplace as a Farcaster mini app. You encrypt a
  file and sell decryption keys; a **zero-knowledge proof** verifies each key is valid before purchase.
  Keys are **limited in supply** (scarcity), **resellable** (multi-hop, speculative), and carry
  **on-chain-enforced royalties (1-99%)** set by the listing creator. Profiles are **self-sovereign and
  portable** across front ends (you back up your own data; he stores only obfuscated, encrypted copies).
  Files up to ~1GB via the mini app. Ask: sellers with files people actually want to buy.
- **POWER** (Max / baseddesigner.eth): a link-in-bio page builder (powerpawr.link). Sign in with
  Farcaster; it auto-pulls your public addresses, links and stats so you don't start from a blank page.
  **30% recurring referral revenue**, embeddable "iOS-quality" widgets (weather, Polymarket live odds,
  live token price), and it's agent-friendly with **x402** purchases. Max's first public appearance in
  two years on Farcaster.
- **Betterment** (Toady Hawk): a "dopamine distribution machine" for creators and founders. Collect
  $1 USDC limited editions, earn flywheel spins for prizes up to 100x; the **BTR token** can be staked
  for daily rewards funded by real app revenue. NFT-utility collabs: a buff in AZ Flynn's Defense of
  the Agents, a Planks ball pack, a free Vinny build. **Live alpha:** holders of **50M+ BTR** no longer
  see "better luck next time" (a karma-cash slot instead); a new **referral program pays liquid BTR**
  for shares that convert. Burr (community lead) is spinning her in-house games out into "The Lobby"
  with Planks.
- **Runner** (Darko): started as the **first Clanker** (a meme), now becoming a real **Strava-integrated**
  running app - log runs, auto leaderboard, weekly airdrops moving to **monthly seasons** with broader
  payouts, a referral program, **Privy** login with tiny ETH gas gifts for newcomers. Built by **Samuel
  Huber**'s team, funded by a **Base grant**; collabs with other projects planned. Internal testing ~1 week out.
- Throughline across the week: **collaborate via NFT utility and referral mechanics**, build the thing
  you want to see, and treat **Farcaster Spaces as an underrated growth hack** (active Spaces are
  discoverable to everyone, follower or not). Closing thanks to JubJub, who organized all of Batches.
